About Circuit Seekers

Circuit Seekers is an innovative project by SeekerLabs LLP, a bootstrapped company dedicated to empowering students and innovators. We started with a simple mission: to make cutting-edge technology and professional services accessible to everyone, especially college students.

We specialize in Arduino and Raspberry Pi projects, web development, AI & Machine Learning solutions, and high-precision PCB design using Altium and KiCad (up to 24 layers). Our platform offers attractive codex, comprehensive code bases, project reviews, and training - all designed to help you bring your ideas to life.

What sets us apart? Everything is 100% FREE for college students - no commission, no hidden charges. We provide minimal support so you can learn by doing, while having expert guidance when you need it. Circuit Seekers is now available for free to all students who want to complete their projects with professional quality.
